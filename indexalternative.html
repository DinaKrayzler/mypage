Dina Krayzler

I work across illustration, painting, sketches, print, and image-based storytelling. I am interested in observation, mixed media, visual notes, process, and small narrative fragments.

This website is structured like a cardboard folder with collected materials: finished works, notes, sketches, and references. I wanted it to feel less like a polished showroom and more like an artist’s working archive.

My practice moves between analogue and digital media. I am especially drawn to visual storytelling, humorous and empathetic observation, tactile surfaces, and images that carry traces of thinking.

Interested in editorial illustration, mixed media, visual essays, process-heavy work, and selected commissions.
